How To Make G-Free Protein Lemon Cake

Description

This is good for breakfast with coffee. The base recipe is unsweetened- besides the blueberries- and I usually have it with whipped cream or maple surup.

Instructions

Mix Dry

  • 2 cups namaste flour
  • 1/2 cup poppy seeds
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon

Place aside in a bowl

Mix Wet

  • 24 eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp vannila extract

Mix in together with dry ingredients

Pour into buttered and floured, deep pyrex pan

Evenly ddd 3 cups frozen blueberries ontop, mix in a little

Bake 350 1 hour+
