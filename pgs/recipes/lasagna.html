How To Make Lasanga

Description

(This is probably not a real recipe and is generated by AI) Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Layer with 4 lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta cheese, half of the mozzarella cheese, and a third of the Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers once more, ending with a layer of noodles and meat sauce on top. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  6. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
