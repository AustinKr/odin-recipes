How To Make Tunna Casserole

Description

(This is probably not a real recipe and is generated by AI) Tuna casserole is a classic comfort food dish that combines canned tuna, pasta, and a creamy sauce. It is a quick and easy meal that is perfect for busy weeknights or when you have leftover ingredients in your pantry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked pasta (such as egg noodles or macaroni)
  • 1 can (5 ounces) tuna, drained
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, tuna, frozen peas, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, milk, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Pour the sauce over the pasta mixture and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the casserole is heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
